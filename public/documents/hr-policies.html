HR Policies

Company HR rules and employee guidelines. Effective as of the current fiscal year.

Working Hours

Standard working hours are 09:00–18:00, Monday to Friday, with a one-hour lunch break. Flexible start times (08:00–10:00) may be agreed with your manager. Overtime must be pre-approved and recorded.

Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to annual leave according to local labor law. Leave requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance via the HR portal. Unused leave may be carried over once, subject to manager approval.

  • Annual leave: as per contract
  • Sick leave: with medical certificate after 3 consecutive days
  • Parental leave: in line with statutory requirements

Code of Conduct

All employees must uphold integrity, respect, and professionalism. Discrimination, harassment, and conflicts of interest are prohibited. Grievances can be reported to HR or through the confidential hotline.

Remote Work Policy

Remote work may be permitted based on role and manager approval. Employees working remotely must be available during core hours, use company-approved tools, and follow data security guidelines.

  • Equipment and VPN use as per IT policy
  • Regular check-ins with your manager
